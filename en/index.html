Common questions

Automatic replenishment
in practice.

The questions we hear most from people who want to stop ordering on guesswork.

How can Listio be “automatic” if I don’t have sales integrations?

Listio uses what you control with 100% certainty: your real inventory. With every checklist, it compares the previous count, understands what moved out, and turns that into replenishment suggestions — without needing a heavy ERP.

Do the purchase suggestions include quantities?

Yes. The replenishment list comes with suggested quantities based on your count and the movement between periods. You can adjust before sending the order.

Does this replace my buying spreadsheet?

In practice, yes. Instead of manually building orders, you run a checklist and get replenishment ready. Many keep their sales system and use Listio for replenishment.

Will I forget fewer items in my orders?

Yes — because the checklist is the full list. You count your whole inventory and replenishment is generated from that, reducing the risk of “forgetting” items due to relying on search.

Do I need a credit card?

No. You can start for free, test automatic replenishment in your routine, and decide later.
